</div>
<div style="vertical-align: top; padding: 3px 5px; border-top: 1px solid black;">Vector targeted. Marci bounds to the targeted unit, choosing a direction and distance she will spring away from it. Upon reaching the unit, Marci lunges to her final destination, damaging and slowing enemies in the area.<br><br>If the unit she runs to is an ally, it receives 35% bonus movement speed for 5s.</div>
