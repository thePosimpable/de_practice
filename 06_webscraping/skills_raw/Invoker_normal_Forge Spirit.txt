<div style="vertical-align: top; padding: 3px 5px; border-top: 1px solid black;">Invoker forges a spirit embodying the strength of fire and fortitude of ice. Damage and armor are based on the level of <span style="white-space:nowrap;"><a href="/wiki/Invoker#Exort" title="Invoker"><span style="color:#E68A00;"><b>Exort</b></span></a></span> while attack range, health, and duration are based on the level of <span style="white-space:nowrap;"><a href="/wiki/Invoker#Quas" title="Invoker"><span style="color:#0066FF;"><b>Quas</b></span></a></span>. The elemental's scorching attack is capable of melting the armor of enemy heroes. Spawns two spirits when Quas and Exort are at least level 4.</div>
