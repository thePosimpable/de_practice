<div style="vertical-align: top; padding: 3px 5px; border-top: 1px solid black;">Morphling shifts its form, pulling points from Strength and pouring them into Agility. The process is reversible. Additional points in Attribute Shift increase the rate of stat change. Passively grants bonus Agility.</div>
