<div style="vertical-align: top; padding: 3px 5px; border-top: 1px solid black;">Invoker pulls a flaming meteor from space onto the targeted location. Upon landing, the meteor rolls forward, constantly dealing damage based on the level of <span style="white-space:nowrap;"><a href="/wiki/Invoker#Exort" title="Invoker"><span style="color:#E68A00;"><b>Exort</b></span></a></span>, and rolling further based on the level of <span style="white-space:nowrap;"><a href="/wiki/Invoker#Wex" title="Invoker"><span style="color:#CC0099;"><b>Wex</b></span></a></span>. Units hit by the meteor will also be set on fire for a short time, receiving additional damage based on the level of <span style="white-space:nowrap;"><a href="/wiki/Invoker#Exort" title="Invoker"><span style="color:#E68A00;"><b>Exort</b></span></a></span>.</div>
