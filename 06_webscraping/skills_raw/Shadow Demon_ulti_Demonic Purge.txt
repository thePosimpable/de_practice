<div style="vertical-align: top; padding: 3px 5px; border-top: 1px solid black;">Purges the target enemy unit, removing positive buffs, and slowing the target for the duration. The unit slowly regains its movement speed until the end of the duration, upon which damage is dealt. Units under the effect of Disruption can still be affected by Demonic Purge.</div>
