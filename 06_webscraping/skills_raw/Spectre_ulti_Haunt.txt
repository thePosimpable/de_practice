<div style="vertical-align: top; padding: 3px 5px; border-top: 1px solid black;">Creates a spectral nemesis to attack each enemy hero. At any moment during the duration, Spectre can use Reality to exchange places of a given haunt.<br>Haunt illusions are uncontrollable, take extra damage, and deal less damage than Spectre herself. They ignore terrain.</div>
