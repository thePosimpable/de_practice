<div style="vertical-align: top; padding: 3px 5px; border-top: 1px solid black;">Grants Normal Punch. Passive cooldown attack. Causes Dark Seer's next attack on a hero to have True Strike, knock an illusion out of the victim and stun them for up to 2.25 seconds, 350 distance and 300 magic damage based on how far Dark Seer has moved in the past 3 seconds. Max power is reached after 900 distance moved. Illusions lasts for 8 seconds. Cooldown: 14</div>
