<div style="vertical-align: top; padding: 3px 5px; border-top: 1px solid black;">Launches a surge of water toward an enemy unit, stunning and knocking back the target based on Morphling's strength. If his strength is 50% higher than his agility, the maximum stun/knockback is dealt.<br><br>Also puts Adaptive Strike (Agility) on a 3 second cooldown.</div>
