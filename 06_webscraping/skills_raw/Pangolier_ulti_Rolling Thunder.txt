<div style="display: inline-block; width: 32%; vertical-align: top;"><b>Damage</b><br><a href="/wiki/Magical" class="mw-redirect" title="Magical"><span style="color:#5B93D1; font-weight:bold">Magical</span></a></div>
</div>
<div style="vertical-align: top; padding: 3px 5px; border-top: 1px solid black;">Pangolier curls into a spell-immune ball and rolls out. When rolling, he moves at an increased speed and can move through trees, but has a decreased ability to turn. Striking enemies knocks them back, inflicting damage and stunning them when they land.<br><br>Colliding with walls or cliffs will cause a temporary pause while Pangolier reverses in direction.</div>
