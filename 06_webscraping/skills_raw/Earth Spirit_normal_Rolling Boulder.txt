<div style="vertical-align: top; padding: 3px 5px; border-top: 1px solid black;">Earth Spirit gathers himself into a boulder and, after a 0.6s delay, rolls toward the target location, damaging and stunning enemy units. He will stop if he collides with an enemy hero or is stunned. If he rolls over a Stone Remnant, he will travel further and faster, and stun enemies for longer.</div>
