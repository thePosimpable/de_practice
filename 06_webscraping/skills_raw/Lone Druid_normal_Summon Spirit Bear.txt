<div style="vertical-align: top; padding: 3px 5px; border-top: 1px solid black;">Summons a powerful Spirit Bear companion that can equip items. If the bear moves 1100 distance away from the Lone Druid, it cannot attack. Lone Druid suffers 20% of his max health as backlash damage if the Spirit Bear dies. Spirit Bear does not benefit from attributes.<br>Spirit Bear has the Return, Demolish, and Entangling Roots abilities.</div>
