<div style="vertical-align: top; padding: 3px 5px; border-top: 1px solid black;">Invoker unleashes a mighty sonic wave in front of him, dealing damage to any enemy unit it collides with based on the level of <span style="white-space:nowrap;"><a href="/wiki/Invoker#Exort" title="Invoker"><span style="color:#E68A00;"><b>Exort</b></span></a></span>. The sheer impact from the blast is enough to knock those enemy units back for a duration based on the level of <span style="white-space:nowrap;"><a href="/wiki/Invoker#Quas" title="Invoker"><span style="color:#0066FF;"><b>Quas</b></span></a></span>, then disarm their attacks for a duration based on the level of <span style="white-space:nowrap;"><a href="/wiki/Invoker#Wex" title="Invoker"><span style="color:#CC0099;"><b>Wex</b></span></a></span>.</div>
