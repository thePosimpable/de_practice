<div style="vertical-align: top; padding: 3px 5px; border-top: 1px solid black;">Fires a long-range arrow with deadly precision, which stuns and damages the first enemy unit it strikes. The stun duration ranges from 0.01 to 3.2/3.8/4.4/5 seconds, with bonus damage up to 180 added, based on the distance the arrow travels to its target. Instantly kills the first non-ancient creep it hits.</div>
