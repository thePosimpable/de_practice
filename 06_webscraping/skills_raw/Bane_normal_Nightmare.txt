<div style="vertical-align: top; padding: 3px 5px; border-top: 1px solid black;">Puts the target enemy or friendly Hero to sleep. Sleeping units are awakened when damaged. If the target was directly attacked, the Nightmare passes to the attacking unit. Bane can attack nightmared targets freely. Enemy Units take Pure Damage Per Second while in Nightmare.</div>
