<div style="vertical-align: top; padding: 3px 5px; border-top: 1px solid black;">Summons a phantom that moves quickly towards the target, latching to it when she arrives. Once latched, the phantom damages and silences. If she survives the full latch duration she rends her victim for heavy damage and refreshes Phantom's Embrace's cooldown.<br><br>Hero attacks against the phantom count as 3 attacks each.</div>
