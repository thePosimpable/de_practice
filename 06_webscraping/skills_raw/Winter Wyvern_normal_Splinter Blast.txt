<div style="vertical-align: top; padding: 3px 5px; border-top: 1px solid black;">Launches a ball of brittle ice towards an enemy. The ice shatters on impact, leaving the primary target completely unaffected, while hurling damaging splinters into nearby enemies in a 500 radius. Enemies struck by these splinters are slowed by 30%.</div>
