<div style="vertical-align: top; padding: 3px 5px; border-top: 1px solid black;">Raises a wall of warping light that slows and creates replicas of any enemy hero who crosses it. Enemy replicas serve at the Dark Seer's will. Replicas last until they are destroyed, or until the wall's duration ends.</div>
