<div style="vertical-align: top; padding: 3px 5px; border-top: 1px solid black;">Pangolier jumps in the air and slams back to the ground in front of his current position, damaging and slowing all enemies in a radius. For each enemy hero hit, he gains a percentage of incoming damage reduction for a short time.<br><br>Forward movement is maintained if Shield Crash is used while Rolling Thunder is active, and allows Rolling Thunder to clear walls or cliffs.</div>
