<div style="vertical-align: top; padding: 3px 5px; border-top: 1px solid black;">Unleashes a fast moving tornado that picks up enemy units in its path, suspending them helplessly in the air shortly before allowing them to plummet to their doom. Travels further based on the level of <span style="white-space:nowrap;"><a href="/wiki/Invoker#Wex" title="Invoker"><span style="color:#CC0099;"><b>Wex</b></span></a></span>. Holds enemies in the air for a duration based on the level of <span style="white-space:nowrap;"><a href="/wiki/Invoker#Quas" title="Invoker"><span style="color:#0066FF;"><b>Quas</b></span></a></span>. Deals base damage plus added damage based on levels in <span style="white-space:nowrap;"><a href="/wiki/Invoker#Wex" title="Invoker"><span style="color:#CC0099;"><b>Wex</b></span></a></span>.</div>
